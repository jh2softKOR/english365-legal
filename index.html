English365 is an automated content tool that creates short educational English-learning videos and publishes them to our own connected social media accounts, including TikTok and YouTube.

What it does

Each day the app generates bite-sized English lessons — everyday words, useful sentences, and real-life conversations — designed for beginners, and posts them automatically to our own channel so learners can follow along and practice by speaking out loud.

How it works

The app uses official platform APIs and only publishes our own original educational content to accounts we own and have explicitly authorized. It does not access or post content on behalf of any other users.
