Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the English365 application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information in connection with publishing videos to social media platforms, including TikTok and YouTube, on behalf of its owner and operator.

1. Who we are

The App is an automated content-publishing tool operated by the owner of the English365 channel ("ENGLISH 365 / 매일 영어 챌린지"). It is used solely to upload the operator's own educational short-form videos to the operator's own connected social media accounts.

2. Information we access

When you authorize the App, we access only the data required to publish content to your account:

  • Your basic account information (open ID, username, display name, and avatar) provided by the platform's authorization (e.g., the user.info.basic scope).
  • Permission to publish or upload videos to your account (e.g., the video.publish scope).

The App does not collect your password, your contacts, your private messages, your browsing history, or any content belonging to other users.

3. How we use information

Account information and access tokens are used exclusively to publish the operator's own videos to the operator's own account. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the requested content.

4. Data storage and retention

Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer and are used only to maintain the connection needed for uploads. Tokens can be revoked at any time from your platform account settings, after which the App can no longer access your account. We do not maintain a public server database of user data.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party. Video content is transmitted only to the platform you authorized (e.g., TikTok, YouTube) for the purpose of publishing it to your own account.

6. Your rights

You may revoke the App's access to your account at any time through the platform's connected-apps or security settings. You may also contact us to request deletion of any stored access tokens.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: jh2soft@gmail.com
